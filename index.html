Objectives

  1. Shelter Provision: To offer safe, dignified, and trauma-informed housing solutions for individuals and families experiencing homelessness.
  2. Rehabilitation and Recovery Support: To offer specialised rehabilitation services, including substance abuse treatment, counselling, and holistic care to help individuals overcome addiction and rebuild their lives.
  3. Mental Health and Psychosocial Support: To provide accessible, compassionate care addressing trauma, emotional wellbeing, and mental health challenges.
  4. Education and Life Skills Development: To support individuals in returning to formal education‚Äîwhether primary, secondary, or vocational‚Äîwhile also providing essential life skills that foster independence and personal growth.
  5. Technical and Vocational Training: To create economic opportunities through skills development and job readiness training tailored to local markets.
  6. Reintegration and Long-Term Stability: To assist individuals in successfully re-entering society by connecting them with permanent housing, employment, and supportive community networks.
  7. Policy Advocacy and Systems Reform: To collaborate with governments and stakeholders to develop and implement policies that prevent homelessness and safeguard the rights of vulnerable populations.

About Our Organization

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a group of social workers and housing advocates, UK Homeless began as a small community initiative in London. Over the past decade, we've grown into a nationwide movement tackling homelessness through innovative programs.
